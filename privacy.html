Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 8, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy describes how Rampart Bot collects, uses, and protects your data when interacting with Discord servers.

1. Data Collected

The Bot may collect the following types of data to provide its functionality:

  • Server metadata (guild ID, name, channels, roles) for snapshots and rebuilds.
  • Basic user metadata (user IDs, usernames) for moderation purposes, such as spam detection and join flood prevention.
  • Bot usage statistics, such as snapshot and rebuild counts, for analytics and website display.

2. Data Usage

  • To maintain snapshots and restore server data accurately.
  • To monitor and respond to spam, raids, or disruptive activity.
  • To display aggregated, anonymized statistics on our public website.

3. Data Storage

  • All snapshots are stored locally on the bot's host or a configured storage system.
  • User personal data (IDs, usernames) are not shared outside of the bot's moderation context.
  • Snapshots older than the last 15 are automatically deleted, unless configured differently.

4. Data Sharing

Rampart Bot does not sell, rent, or trade your data. Data may be shared with Discord or law enforcement only if required by law or as part of a security investigation.

5. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect collected data from unauthorized access, alteration, or deletion. However, no system is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

  • You can request removal of your server's snapshots by contacting the bot owner.
  • You can revoke administrative permissions from the bot at any time, which disables data collection.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy at any time. Continued use of the Bot constitutes acceptance of any changes. We recommend reviewing this page periodically.

8. Contact

For privacy concerns, please contact the bot owner via Discord or through official channels provided on the website.
